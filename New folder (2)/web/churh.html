About Our Ministry

Christ Restoration Center, established by the late Bishop Kirabira Stephen , is a vibrant, Bible-believing church located in Kampala, Uganda. Our core purpose is to spread the Gospel and facilitate therestoration of individuals to the nature of Christ. .

Our mission is to empower people to cultivate a profound relationship with God through heartfelt worship, dedicated discipleship, andpowerful, intense prayer. At the heart of our ministry is a doctrine of "word reality" and revelational Christianity, where the church servesas a dynamic training ground for believers.
